pot·​hole (n): a circular depression in the body/land created by water and stone. pothole is a digital and biannual print zine. pothole is committed to serve as a living archive of interdisciplinary writers, visual artists, and the local residents of the Merced and Fresno area (unceded Yokuts terrority). created by alma alvarado and c quesada in 2026, pothole is their first public conceptual collaboration ---------- we are grateful to our friends' guidance------- and to our future audience and collaborators.
